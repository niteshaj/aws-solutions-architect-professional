# Data Transfer Costs

## Overview
AWS data transfer costs vary based on source, destination, and volume of data transferred.

## Cost Factors
- **Data Transfer Out**: Internet, region-to-region
- **Data Transfer In**: Usually free
- **Cross-AZ**: Regional costs
- **Cross-Region**: Higher costs

## Use Cases
- Cost optimization
- Architecture design
- Data transfer planning
- Budget management

## Benefits
- Cost awareness
- Better planning
- Optimization strategies
- Reduced expenses

## Optimization Strategies
- **CloudFront**: CDN for content delivery
- **Direct Connect**: Private connections
- **Data Transfer**: Minimize transfers
- **Regional Design**: Keep data local
