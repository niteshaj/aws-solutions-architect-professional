# Online vs Offline Migration

## Overview
Choose between online (network-based) and offline (physical) migration methods based on data volume, network bandwidth, and timeline.

## Online Migration
- **Network-based**: Transfer over internet/Direct Connect
- **Continuous**: Real-time data sync
- **Suitable for**: Small to medium datasets
- **Tools**: DataSync, DMS, SMS

## Offline Migration
- **Physical devices**: Snow family appliances
- **Batch transfer**: Large data volumes
- **Suitable for**: Large datasets, limited bandwidth
- **Tools**: Snowball, Snowmobile

## Selection Criteria
- **Data Volume**: <10TB online, >10TB offline
- **Network Speed**: High bandwidth favors online
- **Timeline**: Urgent favors online
- **Cost**: Compare transfer vs device costs

## Use Cases
- **Online**: Ongoing replication, small datasets
- **Offline**: Initial large migration, network limitations

## Benefits
- **Online**: Real-time, continuous sync
- **Offline**: Fast for large volumes, secure
